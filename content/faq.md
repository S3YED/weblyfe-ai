# Frequently Asked Questions

---

## 1. Do I need technical skills to use these AI systems?

**Nope.** We handle all the technical setup. You'll get a simple interface — usually WhatsApp, Telegram, or a web dashboard — that works like texting a smart assistant. If you can send a message, you can use our AI.

---

## 2. How long does setup take?

**Most projects go live in 1-3 weeks.** Simple chatbots can be ready in days. More complex systems (like full digital employees with multiple integrations) take 2-4 weeks. We'll give you a timeline before we start.

---

## 3. Will the AI sound robotic or generic?

**Not if we build it right.** We train every AI on your brand voice, your content, and your specific business context. The goal is that customers can't tell they're talking to AI — or better yet, they prefer it because it's faster.

---

## 4. What happens when the AI can't answer something?

**It escalates to you.** Every system includes smart routing. When AI hits a question it can't handle confidently, it collects the info and alerts a human. No frustrated customers stuck in loops.

---

## 5. Is my data safe? Will it be used to train other AI?

**Your data stays yours.** We use enterprise-grade security and never use client data to train models for other businesses. For sensitive industries, we can set up fully private, isolated AI instances.

---

## 6. What's the pricing model?

**No monthly retainers unless you want ongoing optimization.** Most projects are one-time builds with a setup fee + optional support packages. Digital Employee subscriptions are flat monthly — no per-message or per-task charges.

---

## 7. Can this integrate with my existing tools?

**Almost certainly yes.** We work with 400+ apps: Notion, Monday.com, HubSpot, Google Workspace, Slack, WhatsApp, Shopify, Stripe, Calendly, and basically anything with an API. If you use it, we can probably connect it.

---

## 8. What if I want to change something after launch?

**We've got you.** All our builds include 30 days of post-launch support for tweaks and adjustments. Need bigger changes later? We offer optimization packages or can train your team to make updates themselves.
