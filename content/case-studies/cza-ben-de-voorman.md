# Case Study: CZA Ben de Voorman

## AI WhatsApp Lead Qualification for Construction

---

### The Client
**CZA Ben de Voorman** — A busy construction company in the Netherlands handling dozens of project inquiries weekly.

---

### The Problem
Construction leads are time-sensitive but unpredictable. Ben's team was:
- Losing leads who contacted them after hours
- Spending 2+ hours daily manually qualifying inquiries
- Missing high-value projects buried in a flood of small requests
- Struggling to keep Monday.com updated with lead status

---

### The Solution
We built a **24/7 AI-powered WhatsApp assistant** that:

1. **Instantly responds** to every WhatsApp inquiry — day or night
2. **Qualifies leads automatically** with smart follow-up questions (budget, timeline, project scope)
3. **Scores each lead** using GPT-4 + Gemini dual-model analysis for accuracy
4. **Syncs everything to Monday.com** — new leads, scores, and conversation summaries appear automatically

---

### The Tech Stack
- 🤖 **GPT-4 + Gemini** — Dual-model AI for accurate lead scoring
- 📱 **WhatsApp Business API** — Native conversation experience
- 📊 **Monday.com Integration** — Real-time project board updates
- ⚡ **n8n Workflows** — Connecting all the pieces seamlessly

---

### The Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Lead response time | 4-24 hours | Under 2 minutes |
| Hours spent on qualification | 15+ hrs/week | <2 hrs/week |
| Leads qualified after hours | 0% | 40% of total |
| Monday.com data accuracy | Manual, often outdated | Real-time, automated |

---

### Client Quote
> "We used to lose jobs because we couldn't respond fast enough. Now leads get answers immediately, and I wake up to a qualified pipeline ready to review."
> — **Ben, Owner**

---

### Key Takeaway
**AI doesn't replace the craftsman — it makes sure the craftsman never misses an opportunity.**
