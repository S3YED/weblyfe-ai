### USP Audit Framework

Every stat must pass the **RICE Test**:
- **R**esult-focused (not activity-focused)
- **I**mpressive (makes reader stop scrolling)
- **C**redible (believable, not inflated)
- **E**vidence-backed (can be proven if asked)

#### ❌ Bad USPs (Activity-Focused)
- "50+ voice notes used" — So what?
- "Built in 1 day" — Speed without context
- "5 booking steps" — Feature, not benefit
- "2 languages" — Capability, not outcome

#### ✅ Good USPs (Result-Focused)
- "95% faster than traditional" — Clear improvement
- "40% more leads captured" — Revenue impact
- "€15,000 saved annually" — Money saved
- "99% response rate" — Performance metric

### Copy Audit Checklist

For each case study, verify:

1. **Headline** — Does it promise a transformation?
2. **Subtitle** — Does it explain what was built?
3. **Description** — Does it tell a story, not list features?
4. **Stats** — Do they pass the RICE test?
5. **Quote** — Is it specific and believable?
6. **Tech Stack** — Is it relevant to the target audience?
7. **Challenge** — Does the reader feel the pain?
8. **Solution** — Is it clear what was done?
9. **Results** — Are they measurable and impressive?

### Conversion Principles Applied

**Above the Fold:**
- Hero image shows the actual result
- Headline promises specific outcome
- Social proof visible immediately

**Trust Signals:**
- Real client names (with permission)
- Specific numbers, not vague claims
- Screenshots of actual work

**Call to Action:**

- "Book Discovery Call" not "Contact Us"

## Appendix: Conversion Copywriting Principles

### Headlines
- Lead with outcome, not feature
- Use numbers when possible
- Create curiosity or promise transformation
- Keep under 10 words

### Body Copy
- Start with the reader's problem
- Use "you" more than "we"
- One idea per paragraph
- Short sentences
- Active voice

### CTAs
- Action verb + benefit
- "Get Your AI Audit" not "Submit"
- Single CTA per section
- Make it stand out visually

### Social Proof
- Specific over vague
- Recent over old
- Relevant to reader
- Verifiable
