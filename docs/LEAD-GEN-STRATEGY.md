## 📞 Sales Process

### Qualification Call (15 min)

**Goal:** Determine if fit, identify needs, explain process

**Agenda:**
1. **Warm-up** (2 min) — Personal connection
2. **Pain Point Discovery** (5 min) — What's slowing them down?
3. **Vision** (3 min) — What does success look like?
4. **Process Overview** (3 min) — Here's how we work
5. **Next Steps** (2 min) — Book deeper discovery if fit

**Qualifying Questions:**
- "How many leads/inquiries per week?"
- "What's your current response time?"
- "What tools are you already using?"
- "What's one thing eating up your team's time?"
- "If we could save you 10 hours/week, what's that worth?"

### Discovery Call (30-45 min)

**Goal:** Custom proposal + timeline + pricing

**Includes:**
- Deep-dive into their operations
- Audit of current tools/workflows
- Custom solution design
- Timeline (usually 1-2 weeks)
- Pricing (fixed, no surprises)
