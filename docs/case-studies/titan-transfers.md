# Titan Transfers — AI Transport Booking & Route Optimization

## The Client
**Name:** Grigor Sayadyan  
**Company:** Titan Transfers  
**Industry:** Premium Chauffeured Transport  
**Website:** http://titantransfers.be / https://book.titantransfers.be  
**Coverage:** Belgium & Netherlands

## The Challenge
Create a brand and booking system that communicates premium quality while remaining approachable and easy to book. Titan Transfers needed to:
- Appeal to business executives, event organizers, and discerning travelers
- Differentiate from commodity taxi/transport services
- Enable online bookings without friction
- Showcase fleet quality and professionalism
- Cover cross-border operations (Belgium/Netherlands)

### Pain Points
- Manual quote requests created delays
- Difficult to communicate premium positioning
- No real-time availability or instant pricing
- Complex routing across two countries
- Fleet showcase needed to build trust

## The Solution
Weblyfe developed a luxury transport booking platform with AI-powered route optimization and intelligent pricing.

### Technologies Used
- **Platform:** Webflow (brand site)
- **Booking System:** Custom booking engine at book.titantransfers.be
- **AI Features:** 
  - Route optimization
  - Dynamic pricing based on distance/time
  - Availability management
- **Payment:** Integrated secure payment processing
- **Fleet Management:** Real-time vehicle availability
- **Geographic:** Multi-country coverage (BE/NL)

### Key Features

#### 1. **AI Route Optimization**
Smart routing that:
- Calculates optimal routes across Belgium & Netherlands
- Factors in traffic patterns and timing
- Suggests pick-up/drop-off optimizations
- Provides instant quotes based on route complexity

#### 2. **Fleet Showcase**
Professional photography and detailed vehicle specs showing:
- Luxury sedan options
- Executive vans
- Premium amenities
- Cleanliness and maintenance standards

#### 3. **Intelligent Booking Flow**
- Location selection (autocomplete with address validation)
- Date & time picker with availability checking
- Vehicle selection based on passenger count
- Add-ons (child seats, meet & greet, etc.)
- Instant pricing with breakdown
- Secure checkout

#### 4. **Service Area Mapping**
Visual coverage map showing:
- Belgium coverage zones
- Netherlands coverage zones
- Cross-border routes
- Airport connections
